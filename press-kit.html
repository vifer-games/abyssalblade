DESCRIPTION

ABYSSAL BLADE is a 2-D, pixel-art boss-rush inspired by the demanding mechanics of high-end MMO raids (FFXIV Savage, WoW Mythic, Lost Ark Legion). Armed with only 1 HP, the Wanderer battles five bosses—each manifesting a stage of Kübler-Ross grief. Memorise patterns, execute flawlessly, and strike during narrow damage windows to break free from despair.

FEATURES

  • Single-player Journey – 100 % boss-rush with a subtle narrative thread.
  • Steam Cloud Saves – seamless cross-device progression.
  • Five Raid-Inspired Encounters – each boss sports unique, pattern-dense mechanics.
  • Challenge “Abyss Mode” – post-game restrictions that reward perfect rotations.
  • Full Controller Support – Xbox, PlayStation & Steam Deck.

ABOUT VIFER GAMES

Vifer first gained a following on YouTube and Twitch in 2021 by covering Arknights, a popular mobile gacha game. In early 2024, he stepped away from content creation to pursue game development.

What began as a solo project soon became a family collaboration when Vifer’s brother, Timmy, joined as lead programmer, allowing Vifer to concentrate on art direction and gameplay. The brothers shared a deep affinity for MMO raid content.

The creation of ABYSSAL BLADE was heavily driven by Vifer’s fascination with Final Fantasy XIV’s Savage/Ultimate raids and on Timmy’s enthusiasm for Mabinogi’s raid encounters. They especially admired raids that felt like logic-driven puzzles rather than pure reflex challenges typical of many soulslike titles.

Yet, these finely crafted fights were usually locked behind hundreds of hours of grinding in traditional MMOs. The brothers' goal was to remove that barrier and to package the exhilaration of high-level MMO raids into a single-player experience that anyone can pick up, study, and ultimately conquer—no stats, no RNG, just pure tactical mastery.
